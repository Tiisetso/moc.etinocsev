HELP in using Design-a-Bearing calculator
This Vesconite Design-a-Bearing calculator is a technical aid for correct bearing design. Frequently, bearing failures are caused by bad design rather than bad materials.
The calculator assists customers in assessing the suitability of Vesconite as a bearing material for the application.  It will also assist in designing  the bearing sizes to suit Vesconite in the operating conditions.

Step 1
Fill in the information in the appropriate fields using TAB, or your mouse to move from one field to the next. Fill in both the sizes of the housing, shaft and bearing length as well as the operational conditions of loading, speeds and operational temperature where this information is avaiable.

Step 2
Press the CALCULATE button

Step 3
View the results
This will give the following:
An assesment of the application in terms of the PV (combination of load and speed). This gives and indication of the lubrication level that would be required to ensure a long life.
The bearing sizes with proposed machining tolernaces for Vesconite.
An engineering drawing is given for manufacturing. This is given together with a chart that will adjust the machined bearing sizes to suit the temperature of machining.

Step 4
If you require VescoPlastics to assess the application or provide you with a quotation for the material required for manufacture or the finished parts, then complete the information at the end of the form and press SUBMIT or print and fax to VescoPlastics for a prompt service.
