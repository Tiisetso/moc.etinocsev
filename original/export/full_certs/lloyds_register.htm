STATEMENT OF ACCEPTANCE FOR A

RUDDER AND PINTLE BEARING

Certificate No. MATS/2387/1

This certificate is issued to the company named below. Based on the examination of the data submitted the product described is considered acceptable for use in constructions built under Lloyd's Register's survey. This acceptance is subject to Lloyd's Register being informed of any changes in or modifications to the product and the product being used in accordance with the manufacturer's instructions and with the relevant requirements of Lloyd's Register's Rules and Regulations.

 

Product

VESCONITE

Company

VESCO PLASTICS

VIRGINIA

SOUTH AFRICA

Application

This Statement confirms the above named material is entered in the LR list of Approved Synthetic Rudder Bearing Materials – Group 1(B), Provisional Approval, for a maximum bearing pressure of 10N/mm²

Data submitted

Technical data

Satisfactory service experience at 5.5 N/mm²

Installations are subject to individual consideration

Applicable LR Rules

LR's Rules and Regulations for the Classification of Ships and the Classification Special Service Craft

Endorsements

The installation is to be carried out under LR survey.

The material is to be fitted to the Surveyor's satisfaction and in such a way that, where applicable, an adequate supply of water to the bearing is assured.

Water grooves, where fitted, should be of a U form

This certificate is valid until 1 June 2006
