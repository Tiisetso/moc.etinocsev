The material described below complies with the applicable requirements as given in the Rules and Regulations of Germanischer Lloyd. On this basis the material is
approved as Rudder Bearing Bushing Material
Type VESCONITE
Description Thermosplastic polyester filled with lubricant agents
Producer Vesco Plastics
P.O. Box 47

South Africa

This document consists of this page and a two page annex which is integral part of the approval.

This Statement of Approval is valid until 4th May 2004

Statement of Approval

Germanischer Lloyd

 

ANNEX
Approval No: 6410/99/14261

Product Description

VESCONITE is a thermosplastic polyester filled with lubricant. The bearing material is not containing fibres.

Approval Documentation

Submitted to Germanischer Lloyd Head Office

VESCONITE Rudder and Stern Tube Bushes, Design Manual, MDM 1/98, VescoPlastics, South Africa

Facsimile dated April 9th and April 19th, 1999, subject Approval for Vesconite System and Rudder Bearings, VescoPlastics, South Africa

Our Previous Type Approval No. 95857/90.

Operational Limit

The maximum allowable surface pressure is 5.5 MPa

Lubrication

The material is approved for water and oil lubricated applications.

Installation

The bearings are designed to be press-fitted into their housing. In documentation MDM 1/98 (see Approval Documentation) figure 2, the tolerances are given.

Additional, the bushings shall be mechanical secured.

Swell Test

The bushings are stable against water and oil. The change of the inner diameter and the elongation are below 0.16%.

 

Statement of Approval

Germanischer Lloyd

ANNEX
Approval No: 6410/99/14261

 

Approved Variants

VESCONITE HILUBE:

VESCONITE additional filled with PTFE. VECONITE HILUBE has lower dynamic and static friction compared to VESCONITE.

VESCONITE SUPERCLAD:

The VESCONITE bushing is build up with an outer layer of fibre reinforced expoxy to suit a required housing size. The minimum thickness of the outer layer has to be 2mm

Marking

The bearing shall be marked with the name of the manufacturer and the type as per cover page or above.

Remarks

none

Limitations

Any significant changes in design and/or quality of the material may render the approval invalid.

End of Annex
