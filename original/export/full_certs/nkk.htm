Certificate

TYPE APPROVAL OF VESCONITE, VESCONITE SUPERCLAD
AND VESCONITE HILUBE STERNTUBE BEARINGS

THIS IS TO CERTIFY that the stern tube bearings

Model:

VESCONITE, VESCONITE SUPERCLAD and
VESCONITE HILUBE
Manufacturer:

VESCO PLASTICS (PTY) LTD

have been approved in accordance with the requirement of 6.2.10-1, Chapter 6, Part D of the Society's Rules, 1999 edition, with

Approval Number : 95FF4B

as those applicable to the ships classed or to be classed with the Society under the following conditions:

1.

The shaft diameter at the bearing part is not to be more than 630mm.
2.

The aftmost bearing length is not to be less than those specified below:
a) For oil lubricated bearings:
2 times the required diameter of the propeller shaft calculated by the formula in 6.2.4, Chapter 6, Part D of the Rules or 1.5 times the actual diameter of the propeller shaft, whichever is the greater
b) For water lubricated bearings
4 times the required diameter of the propeller shaft calculated by the formula in 6.2.4, Chapter 6, Part D of the Rules or 3 times the actual diameter of the propeller shaft, whichever is the greater
3. The drawings of the bearings are to be submitted for approval prior to each application to the ships classed or to be classed with the Society.
4. The bearings are to be manufactured in accordance with the manufacturer's standards covering production methods, testing and inspection, quality control and workmanship. It is not required that the testing and inspection are carried out in the presence of the Society's Surveyor.

This certificate will remain in force until 4th September 2005, but may be withdrawn in the case of:

1. Changes in design or conditions of use not brought to the knowledge of the Society; or
2.

Failure in service.

Dear Sirs

Subject:

Rudder Bearing Materials
VESCONITE STANDARD, VESCONITE SUPERCLAD,
VESCONITE HILUBE

We acknowledge your letter and technical documents requesting us to approve the subject material for bearing materials of rudders.
Upon thorough examination of the documents, we are pleased to inform you that we accept your product VESCONITE STANDARD, VESCONITE SUPERCLAD, VESCONITE HILUBE for rudder bearing in ships classed with the Society subject to the following conditions:

1. The drawings of rudder construction using the material in ships intended to be classed with the Society should be individually approved.
2. The material should be produced under your sufficient quality control system and be easily identified.
3. The materials should be inspected during Docking Survey to the satisfaction of a surveyor to the Society.
4.

Allowable maximum surface pressure: 5.5 N/mm²

The letter is valid until 9th May 2004 subject to no change in design or in conditions of use brought to the knowledge of the Society and no failure in service.
