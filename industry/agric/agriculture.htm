Agriculture

Vesconite has solved many bush and wear problems in agricultural machinery. 

  • Tractors
  • Planters
  • Combine Harvesters
  • Windmills
  • Irrigation
  • Trailers
  • Vertical spindle pumps

If you would like to discuss an application you would like to try, e-mail us  or complete our enquiry form.

Tractors

  • King pin bushes for most makes of tractors
  • Bushes for brake and clutch systems
  • Steering linkage bushes
  • Front axle swivel bushes on the MF1105 tractor (20 of OD Ø60 mm x ID Ø50 mm - 60 mm long) (20 of OD  2.4" x  ID  2" -  2.4" long)
  • Lift arm bushes on ACO tractors (4 of OD Ø50mm x ID Ø40mm - 50 mm long and 2 of OD Ø60mm x ID Ø50 mm - 50 mm long) (4 of OD 2" x ID 1.5" - 2" long and 2 of OD 2.4" x ID 2" -  2" long)
  • Pivot bush on the ACO Tipper (20 of OD Ø110 mm x ID Ø100 mm - 100 mm long) (20 of OD 4.3" x ID 4" - 4" long) (30 ton articulated dump truck)

Irrigation

Trailers

Vertical spindle pumps
 Do you have problems with carrier bearings in vertical spindle pumps?  We have done work with customers in solving bearing problems, and giving extended life to carrier bearings.

If the topmost bearings in your spindle pumps run dry for a few seconds, before water reaches there, and therefore causing problems, we are able to help you.

For more information, please drop us an e-mail and we will be in touch regarding possible improvements and solutions.
