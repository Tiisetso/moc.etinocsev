Vesconite for forklift parts
Better than the original

Applications / Success Stories

Vesconite can be used in many bearing applications in forklifts.
The internally lubricated Vesconite gives a long bearing life even when the greasing of the components is not regularly applied. Vesconite has been used in many different forklift applications worldwide. The performance of Vesconite is often better than the factory installed OEM components.

Vesconite can be used for the following components among others in a variety of forklift makes:

Steer Axle
         
Steer axle bushes

  Vesconite replaces the needle roller bearings on kingpin assemblies
 
       
Mast pivot bearings   Valve lever bushes

Tilt cylinder bushes, Side shift slides, And many others.....

Vesconite offers
  • Long life even with unreliable greasing
  • High loading
  • Low friction
  • Easy to machine
  • Easy to install
