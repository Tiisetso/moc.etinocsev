Turbine shaft bushings   Wicket gate bushings    

Vesconite Hilube is an ideal bearing material for the demanding, long life requirements of the hydro-power industry.

Vesconite Hilube was developed as an internally lubricated, low friction bearing material to give long bearing life with minimal maintenance.

The environmentally sensitive hydro-power industry requires components that not only last long with minimal attention, but also reduce river contamination.

Vesconite Hilube eliminates greasing in many applications as it can run without lubrication or be lubricated by process water.

What makes Vesconite Hilube an ideal bushing material for the Hydro-Power industry?
No greasing
Vesconite Hilube is internally lubricated with advanced solid lubricants. The requirement for greasing can be eliminated which reduces environmental risk and other problems associated with greasing and greasing systems.

No water swell
Vesconite Hilube is dimensionally stable and does not swell in water. Bushings can be designed and manufactured with close clearances without the fear of seizure.

Can run dry
The internal lubricants of Vesconite Hilube allow bushings to run completely dry in many oscillating applications and run dry for some time in high speed rotational applications.

Low wear
Vesconite Hilube gives an exceptionally low wear both to the bushing and to the expensive metal counter surfaces.

Low friction
Low friction Vesconite Hilube eliminates vibration caused by stick-slip.

Wicket gate bushings
Upper, lower and intermediate bearings on Kaplan and Francis Turbines

Vesconite Hilube is self lubricating and does not require additional greasing. This is especially important for the high frequency micro-oscillations that occur in wicket gates. These micro-oscillations are so small that they do not spread grease effectively.

Vesconite Hilube carries high loads.

Vesconite Hilube bearings give an exceptionally long life with minimal maintenance

 

 

 

 

Water lubricated turbine bearings � Kaplan turbines

Vesconite Hilube is ideal for water lubricated turbine bearings. Vesconite Hilube does not swell in water and close clearances can be maintained without fear of seizure.

 

 

 

 

 

Trash rake cleaner bushings
Vesconite Hilube is ideal for high load, low speed applications on many designs of trash rakes. These applications typically include pivot points and boom slides that are highly loaded and often exposed to wet and dirty conditions.

Pressure relief control valve guides
Vesconite Hilube succeeds in this application because its low friction has no stick-slip. The occasional motion causes sticking and vibration to higher friction materials.
