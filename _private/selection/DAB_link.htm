Vesconite
No lube, long life bearings and bushes

VESCONITE is an advanced compounded engineering thermoplastic specially formulated for low friction, long life bearings. 
VESCONITE often gives up to 10 times longer service than phosphor bronze, is specified for many applications unsuited to nylon and is unsurpassed in most immersed or wet applications.
