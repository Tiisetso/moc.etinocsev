Bush Talk #23

Vesconite Hilube - the problem solver

When we launched Vesconite Hilube as a brand new product, we wrote to our customers:

Have you ever heard of a new motor car tyre which lasts for 100,000 kilometres?

At up to 300 km/hr top vehicle speed?

Saving up to 50% of the normal fuel consumption?

Requiring little or no maintenance?

This is the jump in performance that Vesconite Hilube represents over availabe bushing materials. Vesconite Hilube is the result of 15 years of research and testing to produce a longer life, self lubricated, lower friction and lower wear bearing material.
It has proved itself in many applications, including:

  • dirty earthmoving suspensions

  • electricity generating windmills with blades as long as soccer fields

  • solving screw conveyor and power station pump problems

  • solving grease polution problems in hydroelectric plants and food processing machinery.

Today dozens of stock sizes of rods, bushings, tubes and plates are available off the shelf.
