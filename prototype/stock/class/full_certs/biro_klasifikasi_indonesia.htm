BIRO KLASIFIKASI INDONESIA

Annex to the Certificate of Type Approval
No.:01.0027P.A
Approval Condition

1. Intended Service

Stern tube and rudder bearings.

2. Description

Thermoplastic Polymeric Material based bushing for stern tubes and rudder bearings. The Vesconite Superclad is built up with a reinforced epoxy composite to build up the outer diameter to the required final outer diameter in a minimum composite thickness of 2mm for bushings 100mm outside diameter and upwards. The thickness of the Vesconite Superclad Bearings will be in excess of 15mm for grooved propeller shaft bearings and 10mm for the ungrooved rudder bearings.

3. Ratings

Bearing surface pressures for rudder bearings up to 10 N/mm² with the length/diameter ratio of the bearing surface not greater than 1,2. The length of the bearing next to and supporting the propeller is not to be less than four times the required tail shaft diameter. Size range, physical and mechanical properties, friction and wear as per manufacturer's data.

4. Standards

BKI Rules for the Classification and Construction of Seagoing Steel Ships, Volume II – Rule for Hull Construction and Volume III – Rules for Machinery Installations, and manufacturer's standards.

5. Service Restriction

Basic design criteria for the rudder bearings are the length of bearing not to be greater than 1,2 times the require rudder stock diameter, and for the propeller support bearings, the length of bearing not to be less than 4,0 times the required tail shaft diameter. In addition, the environment is expected to be generally dry in a housing or in contact with water/sea water, at temperatures not exceeding 60°C.

Comments

a. Type Approval of this type of bearing for propeller shaft and rudder stock bearings is based on compliance, in all respect, with BKI Rules in association with intended service and is limited to the rating as indicated above.
b. The bearing (sleeve bush bearing) is to be designed so as to ensure adequate water circulation and thus promote hydrodynamic lubrication.
c. The running clearance, swelling of the material, wall thickness, machining allowance, interference fit of the bushing into a housing, installation procedure including locking of the bushes in the position within the housing are to be in accordance with manufacturer's recommendations.
