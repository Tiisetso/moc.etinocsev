Drilling large diameter solid rod

  • Drill a pilot hole of �15mm initially

  • Cooling is very important.  Use cutting fluid or water.  This is one of the biggest reasons for failures.

  • It is very important to remove the shavings when drilling.  It is best to regularly withdraw the drill and remove shavings, and then only continue with drilling.

  • Once the 15mm hole is achieved, machine to the required inside diameter using a boring bar on a lathe.

    Example:  A 50mm diameter hole is required.

  • Drill a 15mm pilot hole.

  • Now use a boring bar on a lathe to reach the required size.

Guidelines for Vesconite Bushes in Cold Environments

  • Preferably use bushes with a wall thickness R10% shaft diameter.
  • Use the following equations in determining the press fit:

  • Provide a 30o lead-in chamfer on bush outside diameter to facilitate fitting.
  • The leading edge of the housing should be broken so as to avoid shaving bush during press fitting.
  • Use a press to fit bushes.
  • Note that there will be a slight increase in the running clearance when operating at lower temperatures.
