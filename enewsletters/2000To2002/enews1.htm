 Millennium Sale

The new century has arrived. Despite all the hype, we are aware of many important transitions.

Business is changing, globalisation is a reality, there are many more challenges and threats, but also new opportunities.

At VescoPlastics Sales we are striving to grasp these opportunities, exploring new markets, and doing better what we have done in the past.

In the closing days of the last millennium, our sales representatives visited Europe and the Americas. They found great interest in our special wear products developed to overcome the challenging wear problems we encountered and solved in South Africa's deep level gold mines.

Another challenge was the manufacture of the largest bushes our company has ever produced. Ordered by a United States mining equipment manufacturer, these bushes accommodate shafts over 800 mm in diameter.

As we look to the future, we also look back over the past. Our company has changed, developing new products and new bearing materials. We still have some stock from earlier product ranges, and the dawn of a new century is an opportunity to sell what remains. These include:

• Vesconite GF6 bushing, the original Vesconite grade,

• Polypropylene and polyethylene plates,

• Vesconylon BMS bushing and plate, a special lubricated nylon product,

• Thin wall nylon bushings.

Please look through the attached lists of sizes and shapes. They represent the tangible history of the development of our business over 30 years. If any could be useful to you, this is an opportunity to obtain quality polymer products at these highly discounted prices. 

For your requirements, call Toy Meyer at (011) 616-1111 or Belinda Coetzee at (057) 212-0000.
