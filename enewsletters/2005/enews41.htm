Bush Talk #41

CNN to broadcast Vesconite interview to 1 billion this weekend

Yesterday CNN recorded an interview at our Johannesburg Sales Office for a program on problems experienced by exporters.

To us at Vesco these problems are opportunities: every one we overcome means better products, better solutions and better service, benefiting customers locally and worldwide.

Extraordinary problems need extraordinary solutions. Our mines are the deepest in the world - 3500 m, that is over 2 miles deep.

Vesconite was developed to solve the extreme wear and maintenance problems in these difficult underground conditions.

Today engineers in more than 60 countries recognise and appreciate the many advantages of Vesconite.

Doing an interview that is expected to flight to a billion people presented a different challenge for me.

The program Inside Africa is expected to flight on Saturday March 12 at 7.30 pm South African time (5.30 pm GMT, 12.30 pm Eastern Standard Time) and on Sunday at 9.30 am South African time (7.30 am GMT, 2.30 am EST). For South African viewers CNN broadcasts on DSTV Channel 51.
