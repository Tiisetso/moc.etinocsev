Bush Talk #34

South Africa wins worldwide recognition in many fields

Five South African hotels ranked in the world's top 10 ï¿½ yet another reflection of South African expertise achieving worldwide recognition.

In a recent poll of 425 000 readers of the world's top selling tourist magazine, Travel + Leisure, South African hotels scored gold:

  • 1st Singita Private Reserve, near the Kruger National Park 
  • 2nd Kichwa Tembo resort (a South African venture in the Masai Mara in Kenya)
  • 4th Londolozi Private Game Reserve, Mpumalanga
  • 5th Bushman's Kloof Wilderness Reserve hotel in the remote Cedarberg
  • 7th MalaMala Game Reserve, also near the Kruger Park
  • 8th Phinda Private Game Reserve, KwaZuluNatal.
Six more South African hotels feature in the top 100:
  • 13th Cape Grace in the mother city, Cape Town
  • 46th Sabi Sabi Private Game Reserve near the Kruger National Park
  • 49th Cape Town's Table Bay Hotel
  • 50th Grande Roche Hotel in the winelands
  • 61st The Grace in the city of gold, Johannesburg,
  • 74th The Plettenberg in the bay of beauty.

Travel + Leisure readers considered South Africa to be a safe destination and rated Cape Town the fifth best city in the world.
