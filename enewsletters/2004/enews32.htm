Vescolene Polyethylene HDPE rod pricing reduced

We are pleased to advise that our pricing for black and white Vescolene Polyethylene HDPE rods has been reduced, thanks to the current strength of the Rand.

We have extensive stocks of black HDPE rods in diameters from 20 to 80 mm, and white rods from 20 to 230 mm, ready for immediate delivery.

The costs of our black rods are slightly less than our white rods.

Extra long lengths, special colours, thin wall bushings and injection mouldings are produced to special order.

All Vescolene HDPE rods are extruded from top quality polymers specially chosen for their ease of machining and food approvals. All production is accredited to ISO 9001:2000.

Vescolene rods find numerous light and medium duty applications including wheels, rollers, sheaves, bottling, packaging and food plant components, valves and scrolls.

Since Vescolene HDPE rods are extruded, they are easier and faster to machine than cast nylon grades.
