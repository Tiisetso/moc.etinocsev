Vesconite lifts heavy weights in water

Does your marine business involve winching in trawl nets, positioning a boat using anchors, or even lifting pipelines off the sea bed?

Do you need rudder bearings or stern roller bearings to last up to 10 times longer than phosphor bronze in harsh conditions, with low maintenance and low friction?

Ask Lady Audrey in Durban about her rollers � she lifts 350 tons easily. Her rollers are fitted with Vesconite bushes. Since 1977, Vesconite has been proved and approved as a bearing material for marine applications.

Hundreds of vessels worldwide have been equipped with Vesconite during the last 25 years � ranging from pleasure boats to tankers, testifying to the desirability of long life, low maintenance Vesconite in marine applications.
