Ultrablack Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene plate has been reduced

Dear Customers

We are pleased to advise that the pricing for our Ultrablack Ultra High Molecular Weight Polyethylene plate has been reduced thanks to the current strength of the Rand.

Below you will find pricing for plates, both per square metre and for our full sized plates two metres long by one metre wide, in a wide range of thicknesses from 6 mm to 50 mm.

Vescolene Ultrablack is a particularly high wear resistance grade, specially selected by ourselves for its combination of outstanding abrasive wear resistance, available at a highly competitive price.

Ultrablack sheets and plates have dozens of money saving applications, including chain and cable guides, lining chutes and channels and ore trucks, and wear strips and guides in bottling and canning.

Ultrablack is the best value for money, wear resistant product on the market. We specialise in providing cut strips and sections of plate, ideal if you do not need full sheets.
