VescoNylon Rod Pricing Reduced

We are pleased to advise that the pricing of our high quality black and white VescoNylon rods has been reduced thanks to the current strength of the Rand.

We stock black rods in diameters from 16 to 160 mm, and white rods from 20 to 125 mm, ready for immediate delivery, as shown below.

The cost of our black Nylon rods are slightly less than our white Nylon rods.

Extra long lengths, for example 2m and 3m lengths (to save on waste), special colours, thin wall bushings and injection mouldings are produced to special order.

All VescoNylon rods are extruded from top quality polymers specially chosen for high wear resistance. All production is accredited to ISO 9001:2000.

VescoNylon has high toughness and strength. Applications include wheels, rollers, sheaves, thrust discs, washers, steel mill slippers and general purpose bushes.

Since VescoNylon rods are extruded, they are easier and faster to machine than cast nylon grades.
