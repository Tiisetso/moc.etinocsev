Hitemp 150
The high temperature, super tough, long life bearing material

Hitemp 150 is a low friction, long life plain bearing material designed for harsh applications where temperatures and abrasives are a problem.

Hitemp 150 is a temperature resistant, internally lubricated bearing material with low friction and high abrasive wear resistance. Designed to operate continuously in dry conditions up to 150�C (300�F), intermittently up to 170�C (340�F), and in wet or immersed conditions up to 120�C (250�F).

Applications
Hitemp 150 is suited to a variety of applications that demand a low friction bearing material that can withstand high temperatures, impact resistance, abrasive wear conditions and alkalis.

Suitable applications include high temperature and abrasive water pump bearings, impact thrust washers
