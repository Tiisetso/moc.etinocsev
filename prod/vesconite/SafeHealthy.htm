Health and Safety and Environment

Vesconite bushes are safe to use and environment friendly

Vesconite problem solving index

Environmental impact of machinery and industry is becoming more of a concern.

Designers and operators need materials that do not affect the environment and do not compromise the safety and health of people.

Vesconite bushes offer many advantages for environment friendly design and safe use.

Environment friendly
Vesconite avoids many environmental problems because oil and grease lubrication can be dispensed with. Greases and oils that are required for bronze bushes can often be eliminated, therefore the risk of leakage and contamination does not exist.

Vesconite is self lubricating and so does not require grease or oil in order to have a low friction and a long life.

Vesconite does not contain components that affect the environment during manufacture or use. This is especially necessary in Hydro Power and Marine.

Safe to use
Bushing materials must be safe to use. Bronze usually contains lead which is hazardous to health. Other bushing materials contain asbestos or other fibres that could cause respiratory problems.

Vesconite contains no lead or heavy metals or asbestos that make using, machining and handling unsafe.

 

Vesconite is approved for food contact and drinking water (click for more information)

Food approval

Drinking water approval

Vesconite is suitable for full contact use with foods and drinking water. This is especially required in pumps.

With self lubricating Vesconite, the troublesome lubricants in food plants can be eliminated.

Vesconite is resistant to many chemicals and therefore can be lubricated by the product rather than by additional systems.
