Correctly designing your Vesconite bushes ensures optimal life

Vesconite and Vesconite Hilube bushes need to be designed correctly to ensure that they achieve their desired results. Bushes should not be designed in the same way as bronze bushes. They should be designed with slightly more clearance and press fits compared to bronze bushes.
